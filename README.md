## What is GeoTrack?

GeoTrack is a hosted (SaaS) visitor analytics platform from **Geoverity Analytics**. Traditional analytics tell you *what* happened. GeoTrack shows you *why*: watch real sessions, read instant heatmaps, and ask your data questions in plain English, all from one line of code.

🔗 **Learn more and sign up:** https://geoverity.com/geotrack.html

## What you can do

- 🎥 **Session recording:** replay real visits and see where people hesitate or get stuck.
- 🔥 **Dynamic heatmaps:** click, move, and scroll-depth maps reveal the hot and cold zones of every page.
- 🤖 **Ask AI:** ask questions in plain English and get answers in seconds, no SQL required.
- 👥 **Visitor profiles:** who is visiting, on what device, and the path they take.
- 🧪 **A/B testing and goals:** run experiments and track conversions.
- 🔔 **Events, segments, and alerts:** track key actions and get notified by Slack or email.

## Why teams choose GeoTrack

- ⚡ One line of code, zero-config setup, no impact on Core Web Vitals.
- 🔒 Privacy-first: your data stays yours and is never used to train public models.
- 📈 Scales from 100 visitors a day to 100,000 an hour.
